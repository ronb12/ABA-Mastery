[SLIDE 1 - TITLE]
"Hi everyone! Today we're covering Preference Assessments - an important concept for the BCBA and BCaBA certification exams."

[SLIDE 2 - THE QUESTION]
"Let's start with a sample exam question:
What is the primary purpose of conducting a preference assessment?"

[SLIDE 3 - ANSWER OPTIONS]
"Your options are:
A. To diagnose developmental delays
B. To identify potential reinforcers
C. To measure skill acquisition
D. To determine function of behavior"

[SLIDE 4 - CORRECT ANSWER]
"The correct answer is B: To identify potential reinforcers"

[SLIDE 5 - DETAILED EXPLANATION]
"Let me explain why this is correct:

PREFERENCE ASSESSMENTS identify items/activities the individual prefers, which can then be tested as potential reinforcers. Preference ≠ reinforcer (preference is liking; reinforcer is functional increase in behavior), but preferred items are more likely to function as reinforcers. Types: Paired choice (MS), multiple stimulus with replacement (MSW), multiple stimulus without replacement (MSWO), free operant, ask methods."

- To determine function of behavior: This is incorrect because..."

"The key takeaway: Remember this distinction, and you'll be able to answer any exam question on this topic."
