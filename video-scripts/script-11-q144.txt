[SLIDE 1 - TITLE]
"Hi everyone! Today we're covering Duration Measurement - an important concept for the BCBA and BCaBA certification exams."

[SLIDE 2 - THE QUESTION]
"Let's start with a sample exam question:
What measurement dimension is MOST appropriate for tracking how long a tantrum lasts?"

[SLIDE 3 - ANSWER OPTIONS]
"Your options are:
A. Frequency
B. Duration
C. Latency
D. Interresponse time"

[SLIDE 4 - CORRECT ANSWER]
"The correct answer is B: Duration"

[SLIDE 5 - DETAILED EXPLANATION]
"Let me explain why this is correct:

DURATION measures how long a behavior lasts from start to finish. This is ideal for tantrums because the length/duration is the socially significant aspect. Frequency counts occurrences, latency measures delay until behavior starts, and IRT measures time between responses. Each dimension matches different behavioral concerns."
