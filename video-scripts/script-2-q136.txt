Who is considered the founder of radical behaviorism?"

[SLIDE 3 - ANSWER OPTIONS]
"Your options are:
A. Ivan Pavlov
B. B.F. Skinner
C. John Watson
D. Edward Thorndike"

[SLIDE 4 - CORRECT ANSWER]
"The correct answer is B: B.F. Skinner"

[SLIDE 5 - DETAILED EXPLANATION]
"Let me explain why this is correct:

B.F. Skinner (1938) founded radical behaviorism, which states that all behavior, including private events (thoughts and feelings), can be explained through environmental variables. Unlike methodological behaviorism (Watson), radical behaviorism acknowledges private events but explains them behaviorally rather than mentalistically."
